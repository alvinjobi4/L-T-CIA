Discover Cochin

Introduction

Cochin (Kochi), the Queen of the Arabian Sea, is a vibrant port city in Kerala, India. Known for its rich history, diverse culture, and scenic beauty, Cochin blends colonial architecture, backwaters, and modern city life.

Popular Attractions

Fort Kochi Beach

Historic beach famous for Chinese fishing nets and colonial buildings.

Mattancherry Palace

Portuguese palace with Kerala murals and royal artifacts.

Jewish Synagogue

Oldest active synagogue in the Commonwealth, built in 1568.
